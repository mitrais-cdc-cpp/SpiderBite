About Us

Mitrais is a leading IT company with offices in Indonesia, Australia, Singapore, and Vietnam. Our software development services are the best in Indonesia with development centres in Jakarta, Bandung, Yogyakarta and Bali. We sell, implement and support IT Software Products for Mining companies and Hospitals. With over 400 staff we are large enough to meet the IT needs of most Indonesian companies but small enough for personalised service. In business since 1991 we have written software or installed IT systems for nearly 400 clients. Mitrais – 25 Years of Commitment

Services

Our Mission is to build long term, high trust relationship with our clients as we provide services which add value to their business. We provide high quality software development services for our clients across a wide range of technologies, supported by testing, design, user experience and managed services.

Products

In addition to our software development services, Mitrais partners with a number of leading technology products. Products include mining industry software, hospital information system, and other best-of-breed applications.
